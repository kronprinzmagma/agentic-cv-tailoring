# Senior Product Manager — Platform & AI

**Company:** DemoPlatform Inc.
**Location:** Zurich (hybrid, 2 days remote)
**Position:** Senior Product Manager
**Start:** Immediate or by agreement

## About DemoPlatform

DemoPlatform is a B2B SaaS for media organisations across Europe. Our editorial workflow tools serve newsrooms in 12 countries — helping editors, analysts, and audience teams collaborate on content with AI-assisted recommendation and tagging. We are a Series-B company, ~120 people, headquartered in Zurich.

## Your Role

As Senior Product Manager you own a strategic area of our platform with direct impact on how editorial teams discover, package, and distribute content. You partner with engineering, data science, and UX to shape AI-assisted workflows that editors actually want to use. You translate between domain experts (editors, audience analysts, audio producers) and engineers.

## What you bring

- Several years of experience as Product Manager or Product Owner in B2B SaaS, ideally with media, publishing, or content platforms
- Track record of shipping AI-powered or ML-powered features in production
- Strong technical depth: cloud architectures, API-first design, modern data stacks
- Experience leading cross-functional teams (engineering, design, data)
- Excellent English communication; German is a plus
- Data-driven mindset and bias for shipping

## What we offer

- Direct ownership of a platform area with visible user impact
- Hybrid work, 2 remote days per week
- Competitive compensation with transparent salary bands
- Direct line to the VP Product
